The Beginning

When childhood is about curiosity in probing questions and finding answers to the solved riddles, street children’s curiosity is being driven to search for food and happiness is finding an insufficient meal a day. A group of young graduate students who noticed the plight of street children in India could not help realizing how every extra dollar they spent could have instead helped a young mind spare a thought for a bright idea, coz a dollar could have fed the kid three meals a day. And a healthy body is a healthy mind. A child’s innocence should not make them vulnerable to the society.

Saayam meaning ‘help’ is an organization started to give young minds a living space to think bright and stay focused hoping for a better future.
